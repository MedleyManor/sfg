Companion Rules

  • Tomatoes + Cabbage/Kale/Potatoes = FOES (keep carts apart)
  • Carrots + Dill = FOES (separate in fall rotation)
  • Cucumbers + Herbs = FOES (separate totes OK, not same soil)
  • Garlic/Onions + Beans = FOES (keep apart)
  • Tomato + Basil = best friends (deters aphids, improves flavor)
  • Pepper + Cilantro = friends
  • Rosemary near Carrots = deters carrot fly
  • Marigold/Calendula near Squash = pollinators + pest control
  • Cabbage + Lettuce = friends (share shade cart)
